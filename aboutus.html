About Us


Joyful D’Light is a partnership owned gift shop in Taman Berlian, Anak Bukit neighbourhood located in Alor Setar, Kedah. This business is owned by 4 individuals, Nurul Ain, Nur Aina Nazihah, Nur Shafiza and Nurshafa Atirah. Joyful D’Light specialises in a wide range of one-of-a-kind items, including customizing gift set and delivering goods to the door, as well as speciality handcrafted gifts such as flower and chocolate bouquet and customized scrunchies. The name was chosen to emphasize the feeling of both parties, the customers, and the sellers while receiving order and gift. Since everyone loves to be appreciated by gift, as well as the high demands of affordable gift online in the middle of 2020, we decided to open a small gift shop available offline and online. This business was established in July 2020 and is now fully operating since August 2021.
